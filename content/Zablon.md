A Scholar of great renown he was born into the [[Dawnland]] region of Curse World, and while he himself may be gone he has left many works in his life, some say he yet lives in a far away land still cataloging the wonders of the world, others claim he is out there chasing an ancient being, but wherever he may be only stories remain of Zablon.

Zablon was a famous scholar who collected all the stories and knowledge of the [[Dawnland]] region, he was born around the year 466 right when the [[Age of the first war]] had ended and the [[Age of the Three rulers]] began. Not much is known about Zablon few records of him exist, "*It is a great tragedy that the one who wrote so much was not the one who was written down himself*" - A quote from a book called Zablon The Scholar by Unknown. What we do know about Zablon is that he died at a late age seeing the [[Age of the three rulers]] come to an end during his life and the see the beginning of the [[Age of the two prophets]], witch would mean that Zablon lived to around the age of 110 years. His early life was spent documenting anything that caught the scholars eyes, he wrote about people, cities, history, the magical and the mundane, he even published a work on the bugs of the continent. Later in his life as he saw the [[Age]]'s change and the once peaceful world change into chaos Zablon would spend his remaining years in his unknown birthplace on the western side of the region, compiling his final work called [[The Compendium of the Dawnland region]]. Zablon would pass on soon after finishing his final work the location of his death and many of the details of his life have been lost to time, but his name forever echoes in the bookshelves of monasteries and minds of anyone curious about the world.
