

>  **And so He Created...**
> [[The God of light]] set out on a journey, to create, he created the first plains of the world, but as he created those plains mountains started to rise, [[The First shadow]] had created the mountains and hills so that no plain may last forever, The God of light created fire and warmth so he may rest there, The First shadow created water and the cold so that there is a reason to seek warmth. The god of light created the sun so that light may shine everywhere and then The First shadow created the moon so that the shadows may have a place in the world. When the sky was created He filled it with clouds so that they may cast shadows onto the lands of the world, When He created the first bodies of water The Shadow created the flow of water and wind to move the clouds and make ripples in the water so nothing may be stagnant. From this the beginnings of the world started to form. And from this world that was being created a new force manifested itself, that force would come to be known as magic. At the start this magic was far too wild and unorderly so The God of light used this magic to create the the first ever beings of the world, He took the wild and unruly magic and gave it order and from it he weaved the first beings into creation to help him give order to magic, [[The Weavers]] they were called. And so the world had begun to take its shape...
