Is the religion that worships [[The God of light]], and strictly follows the teachings of [[The gospel]]. [[The Church of Light]] is probably the most powerful force in [[Curse World]], as they have amassed a large following, witch allows them to have their own order of warriors and knights, all warriors that fight for [[The Church of Light]] are called [[Templar Knights]], this allows The church to start crusades against the enemies of the church. The church also has powerful holy magics called blessings, not much is known about the blessings, and while they are rare they often empower certain individuals of the church, giving them insight, strength, agility and durability beyond what mortals can usually reach.
