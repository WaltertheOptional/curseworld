Dwarves were the second [[race]] created by [[The God of light]]. Their purpose was to create the landscape of the world its mountains, hills and plains. The dwarves of the current age are nothing like the mythical mountain forging dwarves. The dwarves lost their power when the god king was created. Today dwarves enjoy to drink, laugh, forge and battle they are also famous for not being the most agile creatures often being compared to a rock. All dwarves have a special horned helmet that proves that they are a mighty dwarf the helmets are given to them at the age of 7 and they add to the helmet as they age, each dwarf’s helmet tells the dwarfs story and they’re history. Dwarves are often named after rocks, minerals or metals in honor of the mountain forging dwarves or sometimes they’re just named rock. Dwarf’s often live to 110 or even 120 and their height is around 140 to 160 cm. They were also once known as the [[The Mountain Giants]].
