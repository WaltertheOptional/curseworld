The Curse World as we call it is a world born out of the death of [[The God King]], and the end of [[The Age of Destruction]], this world has many claims around it some say that the world is infinite and without end, that the regions of the world are like islands endlessly floating around in an infinite desert sometimes coming close to each other, allowing for [[Guides]], to help travelers cross from one region to another, there are stories of people crossing the [[In between]] as some refer to without a guide but I am highly doubtful of this and to see a person who has crossed regions and is not a [[Wanderer]], is already so rare that i have come across only one person who has come from another region through a guide and only 12 Wanderers in all of my years of living. As for the claims that the world is infinite that all the religious follow, [[The gospel]] never states that the world is infinite directly and it is my belief that the gospel of [[The God of light]] is being interpreted wrong, but no one has been able to prove or disprove this theory so it will stand until someone is able to fully map [[The World]].
