The Dawnland region is but one of many regions that exist in [[Curse World]] it is unknown who its first inhabitants were as a great calamity happened around nine hundred years ago in witch all the knowledge was lost and the region entered a dark age. So when recalling [[Age]]s we can only recall what we know after the calamity.

- [[The Age of ash]] from unknown to year 0
- [[The Age of Crown]] from 0 to 147
- [[The Age of shattered crown]] from 147 to 309
- [[The Age of silence]] from 309 to 436
- [[Age of the first war]] from 436 to 465
- [[Age of the Three rulers]] from 465 to 579
- [[Age of the two prophets]] from 579 to 598
- [[Age of fire]] from 598 to 666
- [[Age of crows]] from 666 to 703
- [[Age of light]] from 703 to 785
- [[Age of Twilight]] from 785 to 834
