They were created by [[The God King]] to be his sword and shield, his iron fist to defeat any enemy that presents itself. Now even though generations have passed their purpose is still the same they have an instinct to protect good people with their sword as that’s what fulfils them in life. The people may not appreciate what they do but that’s fine as long as they get to sleep another quiet night that’s all the wolves need. Wolves will be good to their friends and slaughter their enemies. Wolf beasts sometimes become the protectors of cities and villages and live good lives. But people are still weary of them when they first meet them due to all the stories of wolves slaughtering bandits with no remorse and similar stories. Wolves are tall with their height being between 190 and 210cm.
