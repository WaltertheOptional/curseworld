

> And so a Wonderous [[Age]] began...
> The land had started taking its shape, but [[The God of light]] wanted this land to stretch as far as his eyes could see, to stretch ever beyond the horizon, without end, to make this land He would create [[The Mountain Giants]], they would create the land its valleys and its rivers, its mountains and its plains. And so [[The World]] was created, it magic was given order and now it was time to fill the world with its inhabitants. So when all [[The Weavers]] and [[The Mountain Giants]] had done their job The God of light gave them new forms, and along with them he also created a new [[Race]] he called them [[Huminis]] they were made to be perfect and to perfectly adapt to this new world he had created, and so all the beings were given physical form, their souls and bodies were made perfect, but as this happened [[The First shadow]] would add its own finishing touches to them, It created imperfection, Destruction, Disease, Hate, for everything The God of light had given them It made an opposite, and as Its final gift to them It gave them the greatest evil of them all Death, making all physical beings mortal. With this the order that He had created was turned to chaos. To restore order the mortals would need a strong and perfect leader and so He created [[The God King]] to lead all of the mortals.
