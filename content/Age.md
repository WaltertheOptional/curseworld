An age is a way to classify a certain period of history of both the world and a region located in that world, Every region has its own ages that it went through, a new age begins every 100 or so years, meanwhile the world enters a new age every couple of thousand years. While we do not know what the ages of other regions may be we know the ages of the Dawnland region and the ages of the [[Curse World]], we know that the world was not always called the [[Curse World]] but nothing remains to even allude to what the name may have been before this so we must base all of our knowledge of [[The World]] through the religious texts and the stories of old that remain. The accuracy of witch is in my opinion fallible at best, and at worse just has far too many contradictions to actually be true. So I implore you to not take everything in this book as infallible. I shall therefore split the Age section into two categories [[The World]] and the [[Dawnland Ages]] to make that distinction clear.
