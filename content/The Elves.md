Elves were the first [[race]] created by [[The God of light]] the existed long before anything, they used to be much closer to spirits than to the physical created to be the weavers of magic and order. This all changed when the god king was created, the elves were forced to take physical forms and lost much of their powers, their physical bodies were also weak as they were never meant to exist like this. Elves like to be clean and well-groomed and dressed they place a high importance on how one looks and how elegant one is. This is why they often have arguments with dwarves who are often the exact opposite to them. Elves are unique in their looks they are beautiful but they also have strange hair and eye colors like their hair color can be white, deep orange, blond and dark red, and their eye colors can be orange, golden or change color depending on mood. They live to 150 and their height is between 180 and 200 cm. They were once called [[The Weavers]].
