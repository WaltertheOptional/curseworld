

>**In The Beginning...**
> there was nothing, an empty land with a sky without stars a land without the earth the air that had not but a breeze in it. This emptiness seemed eternal with no beginning or end, but it wanted to be something be more than just an empty land and eventually after 7 days, the first thing was created from this want a will and from this a god was formed, a god that willed itself into existence. And so [[The God of light]] came to be, he wished to see the world so with a snap of his fingers he created the first light, and with that first ray of light a shadow was cast behind The god of light [[The First shadow]], the shadow followed The God of light everywhere he went and so The God of light set out to fill the world so that it will never be empty again...
