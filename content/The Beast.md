[[The Beast]] races were not created by [[The God of light]] but by [[The God King]] to fulfil specific roles in his kingdom, he created 3 beast races to fulfil the roles of warriors and powerful soldiers, accountants and record keepers and finally assassins and spies. Today they are the least numbered [[race]]. Due to the fact that they were not created by the true god of the world some religious branches wish to exterminate them and see them as impure pests. Beasts typically live up to 50 and rarely to 60 years old.
