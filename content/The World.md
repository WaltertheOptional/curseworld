Whenever the word The World is used it refers to [[Curse World]] in its entirety and the history that we all share through it as its inhabitants. While The World has lost much of its history I have compiled its history into a simple and easy to understand way.

[[The Age of Beginning]] - An Incredibly long time ago

[[The Age of Creation]] - 7 days after an incredibly long time ago

[[The Age of Wonder]] - Still not known how long ago we just know that it happened after The Age of Creation.

[[The Age of The God King]] - Some books put it at 20 to 25 millennium ago 

[[The Age of Destruction]] - Around 8 to 10 millennium ago

[[The Curse Age]] - Around 5 to 7 millennium ago to present

The 6 [[Age]]s represent the history of the world in its entirety we currently live in [[The Curse Age]] and will continue to live in it for a long time to come, that is also when this world got the name we currently use the name of [[Curse World]].
