Humans were the third [[race]] created by [[The God of light]], they were created to be diverse and adaptable, they are not particularly good at anything but they’re not bad either. Humans can be saints and monsters, no two humans are ever alike so the other races find them interesting in that. They’re also the most numbered race and the God King was probably a human who had mostly humans in his court so the other races sometimes blame the humans for the night of the silver knives and the resulting wars and destruction. Human live to about 60 to 90 years old. Their average height is between 170 and 190 cm but they can be taller. They were once known as The [[Huminis]].
