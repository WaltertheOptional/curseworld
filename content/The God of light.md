

> **O God of light ...**
> *shine a light to guide the bright to a place of peace, where the people never starve, the lover never leaves and the fields of golden wheat never end. O God of light tell them to rest me under an apple tree on my golden hill to see the sun set so I be at peace. O God of light hear this prayer and shine a light down on these most humble servants.* He cried out but his prayer was met with only silence, he would not rest under an apple tree on a golden hill looking at the sun set, he rested in the muck of a field of red on a land he did not know surrounded by corpses and left to rot as the ravens came for him.
> *- A story of a retired soldier named Randal recounting a story of a priest he fought along side with in the [[Age of the first war]]. 

The God of light and his [[Aspects]] are the most worshiped gods of [[Curse World]]. He is present in every aspect of life in the [[Dawnland]] region, even when out of a village or city one may still find the ruins of churches and monasteries littering the plains, He is always looming in the background. [[The Church of Light]] as it is called is an organization dedicated to spreading and maintaining the ways of the light, it strictly follows the ways and teachings of [[The gospel]] a book that contains the stories and words of the god of light and [[The God King]]. The only way to live properly is as He intended and any other way is wrong and should be expelled ~~from [[hidden|The World]] as~~ it is the way of living in the way of shadow witch destroys the soul and degrades the morals and minds of the ones who follow.
