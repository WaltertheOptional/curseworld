
They were created by [[The God King]] to keep his infinite empire running, to be his pen and accountant making sure that the entire empire is running like a clock. Their purpose hasn’t changed much through the generations they still count things but they don’t just count crowns now they also count the amount of bodies they’ll produce when they kill all these bandits with the mercenaries they just hired. Toads are the rarest race in Curse World they are often called greedy and money grubbing and while it is true that they like money what they like even more is seeing people safe and living in good conditions. Toads will obtain tons of money then strategically give it away to improve a city or village in some way and they are paragons of knowledge having an excellent memory. Height is between 170 and 190cm.
