
The [[The Church of Light]] is in reality much closer to two different churches that interpreted [[The gospel]] in different ways, but they both share the fanaticism with the teachings of the light, and are willing to kill and maim to protect those ways. The church is divided into The Traditionalists and The Revisionists.


> **The Traditionalists**
> They are the side of the church that follows the old ways, they await the birth of the prophet that will end the curse and begin a new age, until then the world is chaos and all the races must band together to survive and prosper in this long storm until The Light bringer arrives. They hold traditional views and do not like being opposed, they will help out anyone who will accept [[The God of light]] into their life, anyone who refuses to alight with their views is destroyed in a crusade. But they are also corrupt, their bishops live lavish lives, while the people starve, they often don't follow what they preach, but will put others to fire for the same crimes they commit. The church was not always like this but it has started getting more and more corrupt over the past 60 years or so. 

The Traditionalists are more present on the western side of the region.


> **The Revisionists**
> Around the year 476 a once minor faction in the church started to gain traction, and as it started gaining support so did it become more and more radical in its ways, this faction called itself **The True Church of light**, witch would lead to The Church of light changing its name to **The Real Church of light**, they would keep going with this strange ritual every month or so. 
> The Revisionists believe in many of the same things that The traditionalists believe but with minor differences, The Revisionists are more focused on military and conquest, and believe that the original three races created by the god of light are superior to the ones created by The God king, that The Beasts are easy to corrupt by the shadows and that is why The King was slain. The Revisionists treat The Beasts as slaves and tools of war and conflict, to them they are more akin to objects than living flesh, many of the beasts accepting this fate as they are branded as cursed by the shadows. They also propagate the theory that all beasts can turn into Curse Beasts at any moment, a theory that has sprouted its dark seed into all corners now and has caused much discrimination everywhere.
> The Revisionists believe that a new King of the world is not born but created and so they have rallied behind the baron of the [[Navarin province]] to support him into becoming the great ruler, making the entire part of the region beyond the marching pass Revisionist.
> And so the dark clouds have come over the Dawnland region ever since.
